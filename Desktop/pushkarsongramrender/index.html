FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about creating music with AI. Can't find the answer you're looking for? Feel free to reach out.

Still have a question?
  • Do I need musical knowledge to use songgram.ai?

    Not at all! songgram.ai is designed for everyone, from complete beginners to professional musicians. Simply describe what you want in plain English, and our AI will create the music for you.

  • Can I use the generated music commercially?

    Yes! All music generated with songgram.ai comes with full commercial usage rights. You can use it in your videos, podcasts, games, or any other commercial projects without worrying about royalties.

  • How does the AI music generation work?

    Our AI has been trained on millions of musical compositions across all genres. You simply provide a text description of what you want, and the AI analyzes your request to generate original music that matches your specifications.

  • What music formats can I download?

    You can download your generated music in MP3 format on all plans. Creator and Studio plans also include WAV files and individual instrument stems for professional mixing.

  • How many tracks can I generate?

    It depends on your plan: Starter includes 10 tracks per month, Creator includes 500 tracks per month, and Studio offers unlimited generation. All unused tracks roll over to the next month.

  • Can I customize the generated music?

    Absolutely! You can specify genre, mood, tempo, instruments, and even reference existing songs. Our AI gives you fine-grained control over every aspect of your music creation.

  • Is the generated music truly original?

    Yes! Every track generated by songgram.ai is completely original and unique. Our AI creates new compositions rather than copying existing music, ensuring you get truly one-of-a-kind tracks.

  • What genres does the AI support?

    Our AI supports over 250 musical styles including pop, rock, classical, jazz, electronic, hip-hop, ambient, world music, and many more. We're constantly adding new genres based on user requests.
